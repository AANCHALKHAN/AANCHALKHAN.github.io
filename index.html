About Me

Being an experienced and creative henna artist, I come up with mesmerizing, distinctive henna patterns that capture one's attention and make them want to get one too. I am equipped with innate artistic skills and an ability to observe, which enables me to create new patterns that contain both traditional and modern elements. It could be for a wedding, a festival or just as a personal adornment; the tattoos thereby become part of a story, one unique to each person wearing it. My commitment to this art is obvious in each of the pieces that I make, the response that my clients have, and the enjoyment of life that I bring with my beautiful, fleeting works of art.
